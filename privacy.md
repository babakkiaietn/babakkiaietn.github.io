# Team Shred Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

Team Shred is a strength-training planning and workout-tracking app. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it uses that data, and the choices available to you.

## Data Team Shred Handles

Team Shred can store routines, custom exercises, training preferences, completed workouts, and records you create in the app. Routines, custom exercises, and training preferences can sync through your private iCloud account when iCloud is available. Workout history and connected-health summaries remain stored locally on your device.

If you choose to connect Apple Health, Team Shred can read the recent health and fitness categories shown in Apple's permission sheet, including activity, sleep, heart and recovery metrics, and workouts. With separate permission, Team Shred can write completed strength workouts to Apple Health. Team Shred reduces the records it reads to short daily summaries for in-app fitness features.

If you choose to connect Google Health, Team Shred uses Google Sign-In and requests read-only access to activity and fitness, health metrics and measurements, and sleep data associated with compatible Fitbit or Pixel Watch services. Team Shred processes that data on your device and stores only a short daily summary locally. Google manages the authorization session and may process identifiers and an IP address for authorization and fraud prevention under Google's privacy terms.

## How Data Is Used

Team Shred uses the data you provide to display your routines and history, calculate progress and training-volume views, create explainable next-session recommendations, and provide features you explicitly request. Connected health data is used only for fitness and wellness context after you opt in.

Team Shred does not sell personal information or health data, use health data for advertising, or share health data with data brokers.

## AI Features

On supported Apple devices, Team Shred can use Apple Foundation Models to answer coaching questions, parse routine documents, and help create or refine plans. Deterministic app features remain available without AI. When you separately enable health-aware coaching, Team Shred uses only a compact seven-day summary and forces the request to the on-device Apple model. Raw HealthKit samples, Google identity, and Google API responses are not included in that AI request.

This release uses Apple's on-device Foundation Model. Team Shred does not operate an AI server or retain model prompts on a developer-controlled server.

## Data Retention and Deletion

You can remove app data by deleting items in Team Shred or deleting the app. You can disconnect Google Health from Health Connections, which revokes Team Shred's Google authorization and removes its locally stored Google Health summary. You can change or revoke Apple Health permissions in the Health app or iOS Settings. Data synced through iCloud is managed through your Apple account and Apple's iCloud controls.

## Security and Transfers

Team Shred uses platform security features such as app sandboxing, Keychain-backed authorization managed by integrated providers, and private iCloud storage. Internet transfers to Apple and Google use encrypted connections. No security method is perfect, but Team Shred is designed to minimize data sent off the device.

## Children

Team Shred is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Fitness, Not Medical Advice

Team Shred provides general fitness and wellness information. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition and is not a regulated medical device. Consult a qualified professional about pain, injury, or medical concerns.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when Team Shred's features or legal requirements change. The effective date above will identify the current version.
