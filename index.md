# Team Shred Support

Team Shred is a private strength-training planner, workout tracker, and adaptive coaching app for iPhone.

## Get Help

For support, privacy questions, or account/provider connection help, email [bobbykiaie@gmail.com](mailto:bobbykiaie@gmail.com).

When contacting support, describe what you were doing and include your iPhone model and iOS version. Do not send HealthKit records, Google access tokens, passwords, or other sensitive health information.

## Common Questions

### Is an account required?

No. Team Shred's core planner, exercise library, workout logging, and progress features work without a Team Shred account.

### How do I manage health connections?

Open Team Shred, tap the settings button on Home, then choose **Health Connections**. Apple Health and Google Health are optional. You can change Apple Health access in iOS Settings or the Health app, and you can disconnect Google Health from the Health Connections screen.

### How do I remove my data?

Delete individual routines and workouts in Team Shred, disconnect optional providers, or delete the app to remove its local data. Synced routines and preferences are managed through your private iCloud account and Apple's iCloud controls.
